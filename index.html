Santa Fe Public Art

Discover the art of Santa Fe.

Santa Fe Public Art brings the city’s public artwork, artists, and locations together in one place. Discover pieces across Santa Fe—from downtown to neighborhoods and public spaces throughout the city—through a clean, visual experience designed for exploration at your own pace.

Start with the Gallery to browse artwork by image, or explore by artist or location. Open any piece to view details, see who created it, and where it’s located. Use the map to understand how artwork is spread across the city and get directions when you’re ready to see it in person—or simply enjoy the collection from wherever you are.

Designed for clarity and ease of use, the app keeps the focus on the artwork while giving you the tools to explore it your way.

Features

  • Explore a curated collection of Santa Fe public art
  • Browse artwork in a flexible, photo-based gallery
  • View details for each artwork, including artist and context
  • Zoom and move within artwork images
  • Discover artists and view their work in one place
  • Access curated links for artists and related sources
  • Explore artwork by location
  • View all artwork on an interactive map
  • Get directions to each artwork
  • Choose from multiple layouts and color themes
  • Use the app in English or Spanish
  • Supports accessibility features including VoiceOver

The collection continues to grow as new artwork is added and updated over time, with curated links expanding artist information.
