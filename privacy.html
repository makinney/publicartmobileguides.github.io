Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

Santa Fe Public Art ("the app") respects your privacy. This app is designed to provide information about public artwork in Santa Fe and does not collect personal information from users.

Information Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share personal data such as names, email addresses, or location data.

Usage Data

The app does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party advertising services.

Internet Access

The app may connect to the internet to retrieve updated artwork information and images. No personally identifiable information is transmitted as part of these requests.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

Data Storage

Any data stored by the app is limited to content necessary for functionality and is stored locally on the device.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
